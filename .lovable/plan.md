

# Collections Page — Wedding Photography Website

## Overview
A stunning, elegant Collections page showcasing your 4 additional photography services beyond wedding coverage. Clean white background with sophisticated card layouts inspired by your reference image.

## Header / Navigation
- Brand name "AURORA PHOTOGRAPHY | PORTFOLIO" on the left (elegant serif font)
- Right-aligned nav links: **Portfolio**, **About**, **Services**, **Booking**
- Minimal, clean styling on white background

## Service Cards Section
A responsive 4-column grid (stacking on mobile) with these services, each featuring:
- A beautiful placeholder image with a subtle layered frame/border effect (matching the reference style)
- Bold uppercase service title
- Short description text

**The 4 services:**
1. **Studio Photography** — Professional portraits, headshots, and creative studio sessions
2. **Baby Shoots** — Capturing the magic of newborns, milestones, and growing families
3. **Commercial Photography** — Product photography, branding, and lifestyle imagery for businesses
4. **Destination / Outdoor** — Epic outdoor weddings, engagements, and travel photography

Cards are display-only (no click interaction).

## Testimonials Section
A clean section below the cards with 2–3 client testimonials, featuring quote text, client name, and service type. Elegant typography with subtle styling.

## Call to Action
A "Book Now" button prominently placed below the testimonials, linking to a booking page. Styled with a refined, on-brand look.

## Footer
Simple footer with copyright and social media icon links.

## Design Notes
- White background throughout, elegant serif/sans-serif font pairing
- Subtle hover animations on cards (gentle scale or shadow lift)
- High-end, editorial photography website feel
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Placeholder images from Unsplash for each service category
